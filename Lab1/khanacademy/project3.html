Sandwich

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredient Quantity
Bread 2 slices
Cheese 1 slice
Ham 2 slices

Steps:

1. Put cheese and ham between bread slices.

2. Press lightly.

3. Serve fresh.

Fruit Salad

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredient Quantity
Apple 1
Banana 1
Orange 1

Steps:

1. Cut all fruits into small pieces.

2. Mix them in a bowl.

3. Serve immediately.
